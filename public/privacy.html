Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 11, 2026 · Last Updated: March 11, 2026

TiedSiren ("the App") is developed and operated by EpicTechTus ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal information.

1. Data We Collect

1.1 Authentication Data

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address — used for login, password reset, and account recovery
  • User ID (UID) — a unique identifier generated by Firebase Authentication

If you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive only your email and a provider-specific identifier. We do not access your contacts, photos, or other account data.

1.2 App Data (Stored Locally)

The following data is stored on your device in a local SQLite database (via PowerSync):

  • Sirens — the apps, websites, and keywords you choose to block
  • Blocklists — your named groups of sirens
  • Block Sessions — start time, end time, duration, and associated blocklist
  • Timer data — countdown state for active sessions
  • Device information — device name for multi-device sync identification

This data is not transmitted to external servers. When device sync is enabled, it syncs between your own devices through your authenticated account.

1.3 Accessibility Service Data

TiedSiren uses Android's AccessibilityService solely to detect the currently active window (app name or browser URL) during an active block session. This data is:

  • Compared in real-time against your blocklist rules
  • Never stored, logged, or transmitted
  • Processed entirely on-device

1.4 Crash Reports and Diagnostics

We use Sentry for error monitoring. Crash reports may include:

  • Device model and OS version
  • Stack traces (code-level error information)
  • Breadcrumbs (navigation events, anonymized)

Crash reports do not include your blocklists, sirens, session data, or any Accessibility Service data.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • App data is stored locally on your device in an encrypted SQLite database
  • Authentication is handled by Firebase with industry-standard security
  • All network communication uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • We do not store your password — Firebase manages credential security

5. Data Retention

  • App data is stored locally until you delete it or uninstall the app
  • Account data (email, UID) is retained until you delete your account
  • Crash reports are retained in Sentry for 90 days

6. Your Rights (GDPR and CCPA)

You have the right to:

  • Access your data — all app data is visible in the app itself
  • Delete your account — available in Settings > Delete Account. This permanently removes your authentication record and all synced data
  • Export your data — contact us to request an export
  • Withdraw consent — you can disable the Accessibility Service at any time in Android Settings

Account Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data directly from within the app:

  1. Open TiedSiren
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm the deletion

What gets deleted:

  • Your Firebase Authentication account (email, user ID)
  • All blocklists and sirens you created
  • All block session history
  • All device sync data

Deletion is permanent and irreversible. All data is fully removed within 30 days of the deletion request. Once deleted, your account cannot be recovered.

If you are unable to access the app and need your account deleted, contact us at privacy@tiedsiren.app with the email address associated with your account.

7. Children's Privacy

TiedSiren is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the app and on our website. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
