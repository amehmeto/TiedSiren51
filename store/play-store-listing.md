## Full Description (max 4000 chars)

TiedSiren is a powerful app blocker designed to help you take control of your screen time and build healthier digital habits.

**How It Works**

Create "sirens" — the apps, websites, or keywords you want to block. Organize them into blocklists, then start timed block sessions to stay focused. When a blocked app or website is detected during a session, TiedSiren displays a full-screen overlay to keep you on track.

**Key Features**

- App Blocking: Block any installed app during focus sessions
- Website Blocking: Block distracting websites by URL or keyword
- Blocklists: Group sirens into reusable blocklists for different contexts (work, study, sleep)
- Timed Sessions: Set custom durations for your block sessions
- Strict Mode: Lock your session so it cannot be cancelled early
- Device Sync: Your blocklists and settings sync across devices via your account
- Offline-First: Works fully offline — your data is stored locally and syncs when connected

**Accessibility Service Usage**

TiedSiren uses Android's AccessibilityService to detect when you open a blocked app or navigate to a blocked website during an active block session. This is the only purpose of the AccessibilityService — it monitors the currently active window title to match it against your configured blocklist rules. TiedSiren does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data through the AccessibilityService. The service runs only during active block sessions and performs deterministic, rule-based matching with no autonomous behavior.

**Foreground Service**

TiedSiren uses a persistent foreground notification during active block sessions to ensure reliable blocking. This notification lets you know a session is running and cannot be silently dismissed.

**Your Privacy Matters**

- Data stays on your device (local SQLite database)
- Only email and user ID are stored for authentication
- No third-party data sharing or advertising
- Account deletion available in Settings (GDPR compliant)
- Full privacy policy: https://tiedsiren.app/privacy

**Who Is TiedSiren For?**

- Professionals seeking deep focus during work hours
- Anyone building healthier screen time habits
- Parents setting up device boundaries
